Welcome to Tree Together

We Like Nature

Join our community in planting trees for a better future. Trees play a crucial role in combating climate change, providing habitats for wildlife, and improving our air quality. Together, we can make a significant impact.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a global community dedicated to tree planting. By combining social interaction, gamification, and educational resources, we aim to inspire individuals to take action in combating climate change. Whether you are an experienced gardener or a complete novice, we have the resources and community support to help you plant and nurture trees successfully.

We offer a variety of tools and resources, including:

Join Us

  • Guides on how to plant and care for different types of trees.
  • Community forums where you can ask questions and share experiences.
  • Challenges and rewards to make tree planting fun and engaging.
  • Impact tracking to see the collective results of our efforts.
