About Us

Tree Together is a web platform designed to foster a global community dedicated to tree planting. Our mission is to inspire individuals to take action in combating climate change.

Our team is composed of passionate individuals who believe in the power of trees to make a positive impact on our planet. We combine expertise in environmental science, community organizing, and technology to create a platform that is both educational and engaging.

We believe that by working together, we can create a greener, healthier future for all. Join us in our mission to plant trees and make a difference.

Our platform offers a variety of features to support your tree-planting journey, including:

  • Interactive maps to identify suitable planting locations
  • A knowledge base with expert advice on tree care
  • A social feed for sharing progress and inspiring others
  • Partnerships with local organizations to facilitate tree planting events

We are committed to transparency and accountability, and regularly report on the impact of our community's efforts.

We are proud to partner with numerous environmental organizations and experts to ensure the success of our initiatives. Together, we are working towards a future where trees thrive and our planet flourishes.

To date, our community has planted 1,000,000 trees, contributing to positive impact, e.g., carbon sequestration, habitat restoration. We are just getting started, and with your help, we can achieve even greater things.
